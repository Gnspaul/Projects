Black Gives Way to Blue Album

Alice in Chains' return album after a seven year hiatus following the original singer's passing. An unnamed writer from Blabbermouth writes ""Black Gives Way To Blue" is an honest look inside what ALICE IN CHAINS is today. Reminiscent of previous albums, yet delivered with a newfound level of clarity and humanity that only comes from putting everything in the past where it belongs."

  • Released September 29, 2009
  • First album to feature new singer William Duvall
  • Contains songs:
    • All Secrets Known
    • Check My Brain
    • Last of My Kind
    • Your Decision
    • A Looking in View
    • When the Sun Rose Again
    • Acid Bubble
    • Lesson Learned
    • Take Her Out
    • Private Hell
    • Black Gives Way to Blue (feat. Elton John)
      •
  • Nominated for two Grammys
  • Won Revolver Golden Gods' Album of the Year Award
  • $14.99
