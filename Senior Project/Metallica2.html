What is there to say about Metallica that hasn't been said already? The Bay Area thrash legends have been leaving their mark on the world with 125 million album sales worldwide and more recently with their charity foundation 'All Within My Hands', named after a song from their album St. Anger.Their long anticipated new album '72 Seasons' released in April of this year, and in the 42nd year of their career they are showing no signs of slowing down! The current members of Metallica (from left to right) are:

  • Robert Trujillo: Bass Guitar
  • Lars Ulrich: Drums
  • James Hetfield: Vocals/Rhythm Guitar
  • Kirk Hammett: Lead Guitar
