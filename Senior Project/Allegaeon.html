One of the lesser known bands in the metal scene are Fort Collins' Allegaeon. Allegaeon's songs typically deal with scientific themes and are much more complex in structure than a band like Metallica. This melodic death metal band featuers intense growling, soaring solos, and thunderous drums. Their first music was a self titled EP, and their first full length album was Fragments of Form and Function. Since then, their albums have only gotten better and better, all culminating in my personal favorite album Damnum, Latin for "loss". Departing from their stereotypical scientific themes, Damnum is a devastating 12 song masterpiece about wrestling grief. Though the band has gone through many past members, the current members are (from left to right):

  • Greg Burgess: Guitar
  • Brandon Michael: Bass Guitar
  • Riley McShane: Vocals
  • Michael Stancel: Guitar
  • Jeff Saltzman: Drums
