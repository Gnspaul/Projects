Damnum CD

Lindsay from This Day in Metal writes about Damnum, "The thing that stands out for me the most in this record is the juxtaposition of both well enunciated gutteral growls and almost metalcore-esque clean vocals. Both of which compliment the intricate instrumentation throughout the record. The guitar work alternates between heavy, chugging riffage and soaring solos to classical/acoustic interludes to break up the aggression. The record is high energy throughout but maintains a smooth transition from heavy to acoustic and back again."
